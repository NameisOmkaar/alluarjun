Allu Arjun Bio:

Allu Arjun, popular as Stylish Star. Kerala Fans Calls him as Mallu Arjun also. He was born on 8 April, 1982 in Chennai to couple Allu Arvind and Nirmala. He was in relationship with Sneha Reddy and married her with blessings of their parents on 6 March, 2013.He has a son named Ayaan and daughter named Arha.

Cine Career

He started his cine career with Gangotri movie in the direction of Legendary Raghavendra Rao. He has completed 19 movies till date. Aarya was the first movie to give first blockbuster hit in his career and gave a huge stardom to Bunny. His first commercial blockbuster movie is Desamuduru and he grabbed the attention of mass audience. He worked in two movies without any remuneration and also tried sci-fi genre with Badrinath movie but failed at box office. His recent movie Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo smashed all the box office records and stood at top 3rd place.
